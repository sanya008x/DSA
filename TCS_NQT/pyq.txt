🔥 1) Core Coding Topics to Master (Based on TCS exam patterns)

TCS coding rounds focus on fundamental arrays, strings, linked lists, hashing, sorting/searching, and basic algorithmic logic — often requiring full programs with correct input/output. Practice these topics first:  ￼
	•	Arrays & Two Pointers
	•	Strings & Pattern Matching
	•	Linked Lists
	•	Sorting / Searching
	•	Stacks & Queues
	•	Basic Math & Bitwise Logic
	•	Edge case handling & I/O formatting
